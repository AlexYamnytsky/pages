What is Partnership for Internships?

Partnership for Internships is Akava Erityisalat's new service designed specifically for our student members. We've built a network of partner companies that offer quality internship positions tailored to students in our member fields.

This platform brings together all available internship opportunities suitable for students in culture, communication, administration, business, and wellbeing sectors. You can easily search for opportunities that match your interests using our search filters.
